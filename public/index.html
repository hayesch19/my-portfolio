About me

Originally from Southern California I have set up shop in sunny Tampa, FL. I have always had a passion for working with new and exciting technologies. I spent nearly a decade working in video production and during that time I discovered a passion for web development.

The thing I love about development is there's always something new to learn and it also allows me to exercise my creative problem solving on a daily basis. I love working on projects that help solve everyday problems or just help entertain us.

When I'm not coding up new things you can find me backpacking or camping some new location, hunting down a nice craft beer or cocktail or cheering on the Tampa Bay Lightning.
